Roles of Anubis

Role Explanation
Embalmer Associated with the process of mummification. He presided over the place where embalming occurred
Protector of Tombs Anubis is often cited as protecting graves and cemeteries. The most famous example of this is when Anubis protected the body of Osiris from Set.
Guide of Souls Similarly to Hermes, Anubis was often tasked with guiding souls into the afterlife. This role, called the "psychopomp", was sometimes performed by a god named Hathor
Weigher of Hearts Anubis was also tasked with deciding the fate of souls. By weighing them against a feather, "light" souls were able to ascend while "heavy" souls were devoured by the crocodile-headed god Ammit.
